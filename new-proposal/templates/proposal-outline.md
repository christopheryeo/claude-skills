# Sentient.io AI Proposal Outline

Use this outline when drafting proposals. Each section includes notes on content expectations and placeholder tokens to keep.

## Cover Page
- **Title:** `{{Customer Name}} x Sentient.io — {{Product Name}} Proposal`
- **Metadata Block:** Date `{{YYYYMMDD}}`, Revision `R{{Revision}}`, Prepared By `{{Owner}}`
- **Branding:** Apply Sentient.io logo and Nunito Sans typography as defined in the brand guideline skill.

## Table of Contents
Automatically generate after section headings are finalized.

## Executive Overview
- 2–3 paragraphs summarizing the customer's current state, desired outcomes, and how {{Product Name}} closes the gap.
- Reference 1–2 quantified proof points from the whitepaper.
- End with a forward-looking statement that tees up the Proposed Solution.

## Proposed Solution
Break into the following subsections:
1. **Solution Architecture:** High-level diagram narrative, deployment model (cloud, on-prem, hybrid), data flow highlights.
2. **Key Capabilities:** Bullet list mapping customer requirements to product features.
3. **Security & Governance:** Summarize compliance posture, data residency, auditability.
4. **Success Metrics:** Tie customer KPIs to measurable outcomes; use tables when multiple metrics exist.

## Value & ROI Justification
- Provide quantified benefits (time savings, cost reduction, revenue acceleration) from the whitepaper.
- Include qualitative strategic advantages (differentiation, resilience, innovation velocity).
- Present a mini business case with baseline vs. projected outcomes.

## Pricing Placeholder
- Insert a clearly labeled box: `{{Pricing to be confirmed by Account Team}}`.
- Provide space for commercial assumptions and payment milestones without populating numbers.

## Implementation Plan
Outline a phased approach:
1. **Discovery & Alignment** — Workshops, data inventory, stakeholder alignment.
2. **Enablement & Configuration** — Environment setup, integrations, security reviews.
3. **Launch & Adoption** — Pilot rollout, training, change management.
4. **Optimization & Expansion** — Ongoing analytics, roadmap checkpoints, success measurement.
For each phase list duration estimates, Sentient.io responsibilities, and customer responsibilities.

## Next Steps & Call to Action
- Specify immediate actions (contract review, security questionnaire, executive sponsor meeting).
- Provide tentative meeting dates or decision deadlines using placeholders (`{{Target Decision Date}}`).

## Appendix
- **Product Deep Dive:** Additional modules, integrations, extensibility notes.
- **Compliance Artifacts:** SOC 2, ISO, GDPR stance, data residency statement.
- **Case Studies:** Summaries with industry, challenge, outcome, metrics.
- **Glossary:** Optional list of product terminology for new stakeholders.

## Summary for Chat Response
When presenting back to the user include:
- A bullet list of 3–5 highlights.
- Outstanding placeholders that require manual input.
- Guidance on exporting the markdown to Google Docs or PDF.
